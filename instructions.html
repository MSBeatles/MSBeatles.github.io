Tutorial

Home Tutorial

Choose the first algorithm

Choose your favourite among 3 dimensionality reduction algorithms that are at your disposal. Each one of them is thoroughly explained.

Choose a second algorithm

Choose another algorithm from a different set of 3 options. These are all useful to train the machine that will later diagnose your patients.

Download or Train?

You can download a machine trained by another cardiologist or you can train a machine with your own MRIs. Either way, we will respect your configuration options.

Upload your MRIs

Upload all the MRIs you have available to train your machine. These must be MRIs with known diagnostics. This way the machine will know what to expect from new MRIs. Remember: The more MRIs you upload, the better.
